About

Holy Grail Pictures is a creative studio where machines dream and humans curate. A joint venture between Chromosomfilm GmbH and Lohmann Medien GmbH, we specialize in content powered by artificial intelligence – guided by creative intent.

From social media content for brands to full-scale productions like Wild & Weird, we cover the entire spectrum of AI-driven storytelling. Our projects combine cutting-edge technology with cinematic sensibility, humor with high fidelity.

Wild & Weird

Our flagship series – a nature documentary about animals that don’t exist but very well could. Presented in a serious tone with ultra-realistic visuals, Wild & Weird brings fictional biology to life with the help of modern AI.

New episodes every week on YouTube. Come for the realism, stay for the evolutionary surprises.
